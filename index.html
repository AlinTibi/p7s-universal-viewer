What is a .p7s file?

A .p7s file is usually a digitally signed container used in secure email systems and document workflows. It can include signed content, attached files, or certificate-based information that regular users often cannot open easily.

This tool helps you open .p7s files and extract the real content inside, such as PDF, Word, Excel, XML, images or other files.
